COMMANDS

nslookup – nslookup [hostname]

ipconfig – ipconfig

ipconfig/all – ipconfig /all

ipconfig/displaydns – ipconfig /displaydns

tracert – tracert [hostname]

hostname – hostname

getmac – getmac

nbtstat – nbtstat -n (for local names) or nbtstat -A [IP address] (for remote)

netstat – netstat -an

pathping – pathping [hostname]

ping – ping [hostname]

systeminfo – systeminfo
